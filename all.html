POPULAR

Demon Slayer

After Tanjirou Kamado's family is massacred by demons and his sister Nezuko turned into one, he vows vengeance. Joining the enigmatic Demon Slayer Corps, Tanjirou battles flesh-eating demons, driven by rage and a determination to save Nezuko from her demonic fate. In this quest, he confronts the darkness that plagues his world to protect his sister and avenge his family's tragic demise.

Jujutsu Kaisen

Yuji Itadori unwittingly becomes a host to Sukuna, a powerful demon, after consuming a cursed talisman to protect his friends. Realizing he inherited magical abilities, Yuji joins the Tokyo Metropolitan Magic Technical College. His mission is to consume all of Sukuna's fingers, allowing for a complete exorcism. The series follows Yuji's journey as he battles cursed beings and navigates the world of sorcery to achieve this daunting goal.

Death Note

Light Yagami discovers the Death Note, a notebook with the power to kill by knowing the target's name and face. As "Kira," he eliminates criminals globally. Interpol seeks detective L's help, and a cat-and-mouse game ensues. L suspects Light, who joins the task force investigating Kira. The tension escalates as both masterminds attempt to outwit each other in this high-stakes battle of wits and morality.

Attack On Titan

Protagonist Eren Yeager vows revenge after Titans destroy his town. The story delves into the military's use of Vertical Maneuvering Equipment against Titans and reveals the existence of diverse Titan types. The plot intensifies as Eren discovers his ability to transform and seeks the truth in his hometown's basement. The series explores themes of survival, sacrifice, and the consequences of power.

Spy X Family

In a delicate balance to maintain peace between rival nations Westalis and Ostania, Westalian agent "Twilight" infiltrates Ostania by creating a fake family. Under the alias Loid Forger, he adopts a girl with mind-reading abilities, Anya, and unwittingly marries Yor Briar, a professional assassin. The complexity deepens with addition of a precognitive dog named Bond. The story revolves with fun and entertainment around the family.

My Dress Up Darling

Wakana, a shy first-year high school student and aspiring craftsman of hina doll heads, battles low self-esteem due to childhood criticism for his hobby. Raised by his doll-making grandfather, he conceals his passion until meeting Marin, who inspires him to embrace his craft. Overcoming reclusiveness, Wakana gains confidence and finds love for both crafting and Marin, discovering a sense of belonging in the cosplay community.

Akudama Drive

In the technologically advanced city of Kansai, a criminal underworld thrives among the neon-lit streets, harboring fugitives known as "Akudama." The police plan to publicly execute the notorious "Cutthroat" for his crimes, but a mysterious message enlists elite Akudama to free him for a hefty reward. As these dangerous individuals converge, a sinister plot unfolds, turning the execution into a potential bloodbath.

FANS FAVOURITE

Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

In the country of Amestris, brothers Edward and Alphonse Elric, skilled alchemists, attempt forbidden human transmutation to revive their deceased mother. The ritual goes awry, costing Ed a leg and Al his entire body. To save Al, Ed sacrifices his arm, binding his brother's soul to armor. They embark on a quest for the Philosopher's Stone, aiming to restore their bodies.

Steins Gate

Steins;Gate is set in 2010 in Akihabara, Tokyo, and follows Rintaro Okabe, a self-proclaimed "mad scientist", who along with his friends discovers time travel through the use of a microwave oven and cell phone, allowing them to send text messages back in time to change the present. The text messages end up drawing the attention of SERN, an organization researching time travel and have ultimately made a lot of changes.

Hunter X Hunter

Gon Freecss discovers his supposedly deceased father, Ging Freecss, is a renowned Hunter. Determined to follow in his footsteps, Gon embarks on the Hunter Examination, befriending Kurapika, seeking revenge for his clan; Leorio, aspiring physician; and Killua, an ex-assassin. Alongside encounters with the mysterious Hisoka, the group faces trials, with Killua's failure leading to a dark family revelation.

Code Geass

Exiled prince Lelouch vows Britannia's destruction, seeking revenge for his mother's murder and his sister Nunnally's trauma. Sent to Japan as political pawns, they witness Britannia's attack. Determined to annihilate the oppressive empire that turned a blind eye to their suffering, Lelouch pledges vengeance against his father. While doing so, he faces a lot of obstacles and more are waiting in his path to revenge.

My Hero Academia

"My Hero Academia" unfolds in a world where 80% of people possess unique superpowers called "Quirks." These abilities, inherited and diverse, lead some individuals to become Heroes, aiding authorities in rescue and crime prevention. Heroes gain popularity and are ranked according to their superhero abilities and works, with both seasoned and rookie heroes striving for recognition in a society where Quirks shape destinies.

One Punch Man

In a world of superhuman beings, Saitama is a unique hero, he can defeat enemies with a single punch. But being just one hero in a world filled with them, his life is empty and hollow: he gets no respect from anyone, he displays a laidback attitude to everything and for the most part, he finds his overall hero life pointless... and worst of all, he lost his hair due to intense training. These are the adventures of an ordinary yet extraordinary hero.

Berserk

Guts, "The Black Swordsman", is pursued by demons who are attracted to him, due to a demonic brand on his neck. His goal is to free himself, and his lover Casca, of this inescapable curse. His other life's goal is gain vengeance on his former best friend Griffith, who doomed him and his former Band of Mercenaries, to obtain demonic powers of his own. Gut's giant "Dragon Slayer" sword, the Elf Puck and a growing band of misfit companion aid him in his quest for vengeance

Haikyu

Hinata Shouyou, inspired by a national volleyball championship, joins his school's club despite being its sole member. In his final year, he forms a team, only to face a crushing defeat against Kageyama Tobio, known as "the king of the court." Determined to surpass Kageyama, Hinata enters high school, intending to join the volleyball team, but discovers Kageyama is now his teammate. They must learn to work together to unleash their skills on the court.

CLASSIC

Naruto Classic

Naruto Uzumaki, a village outcast with a powerful inner beast, faces discrimination and scorn from fellow ninjas. Through resilience and determination, he earns respect, forming deep bonds with friends who believed in him. Together, they confront challenges, embracing hope, love, and the beauty found in the darkest times, transforming into true ninjas.

Naruto Shippuden

Naruto Uzumaki, a spirited and aspiring ninja, seeks approval and dreams of becoming the revered Hokage, the village leader. Faced with the looming threat of the mysterious Akatsuki organization, Naruto embarks on intense training, determined to push his limits and strengthen his skills to protect his village from impending danger.

One Piece

Gol D. Roger, the Pirate King, reveals the existence of the elusive treasure, One Piece, just before his execution. This declaration sparks a global pursuit of the treasure among aspiring pirates. Twenty-two years later, Monkey D. Luffy sets out on his own pirate journey, determined to find One Piece and claim the title of the next Pirate King.

Bleach

Ichigo, a high schooler with the ability to see ghosts, has his life altered when he and his sisters are attacked by a Hollow. Shinigami Rukia steps in, transferring her powers to Ichigo. Now a reluctant Shinigami, Ichigo must navigate his duties to send spirits to Soul Society and combat Hollows, preserving balance between the living and the dead.
