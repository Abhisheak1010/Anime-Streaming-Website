JUJUTSU KAISEN
Description:

In the realm of "Jujutsu Kaisen," Jujutsu Sorcerers, also known as "Cursed Technique Masters" or "Shamans," wield control over Cursed Energy within their bodies. This mastery grants them the ability to manipulate and reduce the release of Cursed Energy at will. Distinguished practitioners, both among sorcerers and curses, refine this energy to execute personalized and often familial Cursed Techniques. A pinnacle of this arcane prowess lies in Domain Expansion, where sorcerers construct pocket dimensions of varying sizes. Within these domains, attacks consistently connect and intensify, revealing a complex and strategic dimension to their supernatural combat techniques. The series delves deep into the intricate dynamics of this unique metaphysical world.
