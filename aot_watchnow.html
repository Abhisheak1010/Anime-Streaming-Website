Attack on Titan

Original title: Shingeki no Kyojin

TV Series 2013–2023 TV-MA 24m

IMDB RATING:
9.1/10

In a world devastated by Titans—giant, pleasure-driven creatures that devour humanity—remaining survivors find refuge behind towering walls. Eren and Mikasa, after witnessing the annihilation of their city by a colossal titan, endure the loss of Eren's mother. With titans breaching the once-secure walls, Eren pledges to annihilate every titan and avenge humanity's suffering, setting the stage for a tale of survival, vengeance, and the relentless fight against monstrous adversaries.
