Kingdoms of ruin

For ages, humans and witches had an alliance with witches using magic to help people. However, when humans eventually develop science that can at times outdo magic, the Redia Empire suddenly declares witches obsolete and orders them eradicated. The witch Chloe raised the human orphan Adonis and taught him her craft. When she is caught and executed, Adonis vows revenge against humanity.

Koutetsu Sangokushi

The story takes place in the ancient era. In the old times, in a time of chaos and civil war, the Imperial Seal grants immense power to chosen warriors. Rikuson, reluctant heir to the guardians, seeks to recover the stolen Seal from Sonsaku. When Sonsaku is assassinated, Rikuson embarks on a journey to reclaim the Seal, carrying the will of Sonsaku and thus unraveling his destiny in a tale of epic battles, loss, and self-discovery.

Blue Lock

Yoichi Isagi, a high school soccer player, who wants to become the best football player in the world, faces regret for a split-second decision that cost his team a nationals spot. Invited to the controversial Blue Lock project by the Japan Football Union, Isagi competes against 299 U-18 strikers to become the nation's star player. Despite ethical concerns, he strives ruthlessly to reach the top, driven by a desire for redemption and success.

Undead Unluck

Fuuko Izumo, plagued by a decade-long curse causing misfortune to those around her, contemplates suicide after concluding her favorite manga. However, a strange man, Andy, touches her, revealing his immortality. United by a shared desire for death, Fuuko and Andy form an unconventional alliance. Yet, a shadowy organization seeks to exploit their unique abilities, adding a layer of mystery to their journey.

Oshi No Ko

In the entertainment industry, celebrities project idealized personas, shielding their true selves from the public. The Fans invest unwavering support . Sixteen-year-old pop idol Ai Hoshino takes a hiatus, causing concern. Born with extreme beauty and astonishing voice, Ai becomes very popular. Gorou Amemiya, a so called fan of Ai, dreams of meeting her. His wish comes true when Ai, who's is pregnant, visits his hospital. Promising to ensure a safe delivery, Amemiya wonders how this encounter will alter their connection.
