OSHI NO KO
Description:

In the entertainment industry, celebrities project idealized personas, shielding their true selves from the public. The Fans invest unwavering support . Sixteen-year-old pop idol Ai Hoshino takes a hiatus, causing concern. Born with extreme beauty and astonishing voice, Ai becomes very popular. Gorou Amemiya, a so called fan of Ai, dreams of meeting her. His wish comes true when Ai, who's is pregnant, visits his hospital. Promising to ensure a safe delivery, Amemiya wonders how this encounter will alter their connection.
