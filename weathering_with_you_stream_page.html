Weathering With You

Original title: Tenki no ko
2019 . PG-13 . 1h 52 min
IMDB : 7.5/10

Hodaka Morishima, a high schooler, escapes to Tokyo and encounters a mysterious girl, Hina Amano, with the ability to control the weather. Together, they start a business offering to clear the sky for special events. However, their success attracts unwanted attention, leading to a series of challenges. As the authorities close in, Hina's unique power comes at a cost, and the two find themselves entangled in a heart-wrenching dilemma. The story explores love, sacrifice, and the consequences of altering the natural order.The narrative weaves a captivating tale of youth, destiny, and the indomitable spirit to defy the forces of nature.
